NLTT 11748: A Unique Double White Dwarf


This movie shows an exaggeration of what happens when the smaller white dwarf passes in front of the larger one. Not only is some of the light blocked, but the smaller white dwarf also bends the light around it, distorting and magnifying the larger white dwarf. The magnification here has been increased by a factor of 10 compared to reality to make it more visible, and the green circle shows the original size of the white dwarf before magnification.
